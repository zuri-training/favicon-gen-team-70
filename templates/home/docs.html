Getting Started with Iconcity

What is Iconcity?

Iconcity is a unique product responsible for generating favicons. Favicons are what allows your webpages to stand out from other web pages. It can be noticed as an icon visible next in the title of tabs, a bookmark icon and many others.

What distinguishes us from others?

ICONCITY will provide a user friendly, and responsive website with robust documentation and community where designers and developers can collaborate and provide clients with a unique user experience

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Iconcity free to use?

    Yes, Iconcity is completely free to use

    Can I redownload a saved favicon?

    Yes, you get the option of redownloading a saved favicon(avaiable to registered users)

    Why isn't my favicon displaying?

    Web browsers cache the favicon and sometimes display older versions. You may need to clear your cache if seems like your favicon is not updating. This will force the browser to display the latest version of your favicon.
